Get to know about Vanavihari

Welcome to Vanavihari, where Community Based Eco-Tourism (CBET) thrives under the ownership of the Andhra Pradesh Forest Department. Our retreats are lovingly managed by local tribal communities, offering them not just employment but also a deep sense of belonging and pride. A portion of the proceeds from our resorts goes directly to the tribal members, ensuring sustainable livelihoods and security. With the Konda Reddy and Valmiki tribes at the heart of our endeavors, Vanavihari boasts two remarkable retreats: one nestled in Maredumilli and the other, Jungle Star Eco Camp, situated in Valammuru. We extend our heartfelt thanks to the diligent efforts of our DFO and Range Officers, who tirelessly safeguard both the forest and the well-being of our community. Welcome to a journey of nature, culture, and responsible tourism at Vanavihari.

Our Resorts

Vanavihari, Maredumilli

Vanavihari resort, nestled in Maredumilli, is a haven for eco-tourism enthusiasts with its bamboo-filled surroundings. This community-centric guesthouse offers a peaceful escape amidst nature, providing cozy cottages and easy access to street food restaurants offering bamboo biriyani. A stroll outside reveals a heavenly experience.

Jungle Star, Valamuru

Jungle Star Eco Camp offers a unique island-like setting with seven cottages perched on a hillock, accessed only by a bridge. Crossing the hanging bridge is thrilling as it sways over the flowing river below. Surrounded by towering mountains, the camp provides stunning views. Managed by the Andhra Pradesh Forest Department, it offers eco-friendly adventures like treks and campfires in the Eastern Ghats, promising an unforgettable stay in nature.
