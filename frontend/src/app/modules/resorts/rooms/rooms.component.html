About {{ selectedResortInfo.title }}

Vanavihari is a serene eco-tourism guesthouse nestled in the bamboo-rich forests of Maredumilli. Designed to support local communities and responsible tourism, it offers a peaceful stay close to nature.

Located near Amruthadhara Waterfalls, the property features comfortable cottages and rooms, making it ideal for nature lovers seeking a calm and immersive forest experience. Early morning and evening walks offer a truly refreshing escape.

Jungle Star Eco Camp offers a unique island-like setting atop a hillock, surrounded by water and accessible only via a hanging bridge.

Ideal for adventure lovers, it features river-view cottages and immersive nature experiences. Guests can enjoy river treks, campfires, and a complete eco-friendly retreat.
