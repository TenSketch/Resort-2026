Seasonal Waterfall

Jalatarangini Waterfall

Maredumilli - Bhadrachalam Highway (7 km from Bus Stand)

A picturesque cascade amidst lush forest and rocky terrain. This multi-tier waterfall comes alive during the monsoon, offering a crystal-clear gush of water. It requires a short 150m walk from the entry point, making it accessible yet adventurous.

Amruthadhara Waterfall

15 km from Maredumilli Bus Stand

A stunning, two-stage perennial waterfall formed over a seasonal stream amidst steep rocks. While the 1 km trek down is steep and requires about an hour, the breathtaking view of the milky white water cascading down makes it a nature lover's paradise.

Soft Trek

Jalatarangi to G.M.Valasa Trek

Starts at Jalatarangini Waterfalls

An easy 3.5 km trek suitable for beginners, winding through diverse flora and fauna. Experience the thrill of the jungle with chances to spot Indian giant squirrels, hornbills, and peacocks. The trail is a one-way path ending at G.M. Valasa.

10 km from Maredumilli

A challenging 7 km trek for experienced adventurers. Starting from the island-like Jungle Star Eco Camp, the trail involves crossing water via a bridge and ascending a slippery, strenuous path to the tribal village of Nellore.
