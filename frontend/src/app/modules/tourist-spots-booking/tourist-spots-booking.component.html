About Vanavihari Booking

Vanavihari is a serene eco-tourism guesthouse nestled in the bamboo-rich forests of Maredumilli. Designed to support local communities and responsible tourism, it offers a peaceful stay close to nature.

Located near Amruthadhara Waterfalls, the property features comfortable cottages and rooms, making it ideal for nature lovers seeking a calm and immersive forest experience.

Trek Guidelines for Visitors

  • Trek operational hours starts 6:00 AM and ends at 3:00 PM.
  • Trek slots are allotted by Forest/Admin Officers.
  • Plastic, smoking, alcohol, and drugs are not allowed.
  • Wear proper trekking shoes.
  • Follow all safety rules and guide instructions at all times.
  • Please avoid wearing makeup during trekking.
  • Treks are conducted only with authorized guides.
