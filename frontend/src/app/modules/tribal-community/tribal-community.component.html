Konda Reddi

Godavari River Banks & Hilly Regions

The Konda Reddis are a prominent tribal group inhabiting the banks on either side of the Godavari River. Native to the hilly and forested regions of East and West Godavari and Khammam districts, they have preserved their unique culture and traditions for centuries.

Valmiki

Visakhapatnam & East Godavari Agency

Recognized as the only Scheduled Tribe in the Agency tracts of Andhra Pradesh, the Valmikis claim descent from the sage Valmiki, author of the Ramayana. They are primarily found in the agency areas of Visakhapatnam and East Godavari districts and are divided into various 'gotrams' that regulate their social institutions.
